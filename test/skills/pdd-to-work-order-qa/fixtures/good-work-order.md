# Work Order Agreement #WO-2026-001
## Malaria ITN Distribution Verification

| Work Order Number | WO-2026-001 |
|---|---|
| Work Order Date | 2026-05-22 |
| Work Order Title | Malaria ITN Distribution Verification |
| Period of Performance | 2026-05-22 to 2026-07-31 |

## 1. Background

Dimagi is delivering the CRISPR-Connect platform for malaria ITN distribution verification. This Work Order engages a Partner organization to deploy FLWs in the field.

## 2. Scope of Work

The Partner will deploy FLWs to conduct atomic-visit verifications at household level. Each FLW completes one verification per visit, capturing a geo-tagged photo and GPS coordinates of the distributed ITN. Visits are atomic — one household per CommCare form submission, no follow-up required.

## 3. Geographic Coverage

The Partner will cover three districts in Sokoto State, Nigeria: Wamakko, Bodinga, and Tureta. Approximately 500 households across these districts.

## 4. Deliverables and Verification

### 4.1 Primary Deliverable

500 verified ITN distribution visits, each consisting of a CommCare form submission with a photo of the deployed ITN and GPS coordinates within the target household area.

### 4.2 Definition of a Verified Unit

A verified unit is a CommCare submission that passes all three evidence layers:
- Layer A: GPS within 100m of registered household
- Layer B: Photo passes multimedia AI judge for ITN visibility
- Layer C: No duplicate submissions within 48 hours

### 4.3 Reporting Deliverables

The Partner will deliver weekly progress reports via the Connect Labs dashboard, plus a final close-out summary at the end of the Period of Performance.

## 5. Timeline and Milestones

| Milestone | Target Date | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Kickoff | 2026-05-22 | FLW training complete |
| Midpoint | 2026-06-26 | 250 verified visits |
| Closeout | 2026-07-31 | 500 verified visits + final report |

The Partner will flag any timeline risk in writing to Dimagi within 24 hours of identification.

## 6. Payment Terms

### 6.1 Total Not-to-Exceed

Dimagi's total financial commitment under this Work Order is USD 2500, not-to-exceed, inclusive of all field collection costs, FLW compensation, supervision, transport, and partner reporting time.

### 6.2 Payment Schedule

| Milestone | % of Total | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Midpoint | 40% | 250 verified visits |
| Closeout | 60% | 500 verified visits + final report |

Dimagi will pay only for verified units.

## 7. Roles and Responsibilities

| Activity | Partner | Dimagi |
|---|---|---|
| FLW recruitment | R | C |
| Field supervision | R | I |
| Platform support | C | R |
| Verification audit | I | R |

## 8. Permissions, Ethics, and Compliance

### 8.1 Permissions

The Partner has secured written permission from the Sokoto State Ministry of Health and each Local Government Authority covering the three target districts.

### 8.2 Ethics

The Partner will obtain informed verbal consent from each household head prior to ITN verification. No PII beyond GPS coordinates and household identifier is collected.

### 8.3 Security and Duty of Care

The Partner retains sole responsibility for the safety and security of its field teams. The Partner will not deploy FLWs to areas where the prevailing security situation, in the Partner's professional judgement, poses an unacceptable risk.

## 9. Data Handling

| Data Class | Storage | Retention | Access |
|---|---|---|---|
| GPS coordinates | CommCare HQ | 5 years | Partner + Dimagi |
| Photos | CommCare HQ | 5 years | Partner + Dimagi |
| Aggregate reports | Connect Labs | Indefinite | Partner + Dimagi + LLO |
